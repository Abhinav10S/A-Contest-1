The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a bread of small scent hound , similar in appearanc to the much larger foxhound . The Beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare , known as beagling . Possessing a grat sense of smell and superior tracking instincts , the Beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in a quarsntine around the world. The Beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size , good temper , ans a lack of inherited health problems.
